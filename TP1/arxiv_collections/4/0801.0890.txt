In this paper the author maintains that the Standard Cosmology is not a
Physical Theory. The Standard Cosmology is a narration similar to the
historical or mythical narratives constructed from the Physical Sciences data.
Also the author maintains that this theory is founded on Philosophical
principles which are aliens to the Physical Theory, as the Principle of
Sufficient Reason. The author porpoise a new cosmological model based more on
eventuality than in necessity.
