What are the differences and similarities between atomic-physics studies at
different peoples (Han, Kazak and Uygur perples in the same university) across
Xinjiang (a far-west district in PR China which is a border for previous USSR
and Kazak)? In this short report we focus on issues relating to the learning
style of different-people students to pass the atomic physics course in physics
department even the quantum mechanics course has not been taken before.
