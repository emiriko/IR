We study transitions between Upsilon states with the emission of charged
pions using 604.6/fb of data collected with the Belle detector at the KEKB
asymmetric-energy e+e- collider. The measured product branching fraction is
B(Upsilon(4S) -> Upsilon(1S)pi+pi-) * B(Upsilon(1S) -> mu+mu-) = = (2.11 +/-
0.30(stat.) +/- 0.14(sys.))*10^(-6) and the partial decay width is
Gamma(Upsilon(4S) -> Upsilon(1S)pi+pi-) = = (1.75 +/- 0.25(stat.) +/-
0.24(sys.)) keV.
