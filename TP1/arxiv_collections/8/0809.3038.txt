In this article, we calculate the masses and the pole residues of the
${1/2}^+$ heavy baryons $\Omega_c^0(css)$ and $\Omega_b^-(bss)$ with the QCD
sum rules. The numerical values $M_{\Omega_c^0}=(2.72\pm0.18) \rm{GeV}$ (or
$M_{\Omega_c^0}=(2.71\pm0.18) \rm{GeV}$) and $M_{\Omega_b^-}=(6.13\pm0.12)
\rm{GeV}$ (or $M_{\Omega_b^-}=(6.18\pm0.13) \rm{GeV}$) are in good agreement
with the experimental data.
