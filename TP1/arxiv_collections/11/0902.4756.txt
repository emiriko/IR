The February 2009 edition of the AAO newsletter contains articles on the
preliminary results from the WiggleZ dark energy survey, the analysis of
near-IR observations of the galaxy IRAS18293-3413, the early results from the
SPIRAL IFU investigation of the dynamics of a sample of nearby star forming
galaxies, a summary of the successful outcome of the first on-sky test of
photonic OH suppression and a number of regular features.
