This is a collection of 1031 formulas that were generated by a computer
program in 1992. The set is the database of integer sequences as of 1992 which
contained 4568 sequences. These sequences were later published in the
Encyclopedia of Integer Sequences by Neil J.A. Sloane and Simon Plouffe. The
text explain the methodology used for finding a formula. The rest of the pages
(over 500) is the collection of formulas found. NOTE : the sequences are
numbered in the old system.
