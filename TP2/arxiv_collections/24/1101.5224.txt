} In this article, we put forward a Neumann eigenvalue problem for the
bi-harmonic operator $\Delta^2$ on a bounded smooth domain $\Om$ in the
Euclidean $n$-space ${\bf R}^n$ ($n\ge2$) and then prove that the corresponding
first non-zero eigenvalue $\Upsilon_1(\Om)$ admits the isoperimetric inequality
of Szeg\"o-Weinberger type: $\Upsilon_1(\Om)\le \Upsilon_1(B_{\Om})$, where
$B_{\Om}$ is a ball in ${\bf R}^n$ with the same volume of $\Om$. The
isoperimetric inequality of Szeg\"o-Weinberger type for the first nonzero
Neumann eigenvalue of the even-multi-Laplacian operators $\Delta^{2m}$
($m\ge1$) on $\Om$ is also exploited.
