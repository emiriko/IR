We report results of measurement of the unitarity triangle angle $\phi_3$
with $B^+\rightarrow D^{(*)}K^+$ Dalitz plot analysis and related issues with
$B^0 \rightarrow D_s^{*+} \pi^-$ and $B^0 \rightarrow D_s^{*-} K^+$ decay
processes. The Dalitz analysis improves accuracy of the angle $\phi_3$ as
$78.4^\circ ~^{+10.8^\circ}_{-11.6^\circ}\pm 3.6^\circ\pm 8.9^\circ$ and the
branching fractions are found to be $B^0 \rightarrow D_s^{*+} \pi^-(K^-)$
decays are set as $\mathcal{B}(B^0 \rightarrow D_s^{*+} \pi^-) = (1.75 \pm 0.34
(\mathrm{stat}) \pm 0.17(\mathrm{syst}) \pm 0.11(\mathcal{B})) \times 10^{-5}$
and $\mathcal{B}(B^0 \rightarrow D_s^{*-} K^+) = (2.02 \pm 0.33 (\mathrm{stat})
\pm 0.18({\rm syst}) \pm 0.13(\mathcal{B})) \times 10^{-5}$.
