Let $X$ be an algebraic variety, $f$ a regular function, $j:U\subset X$ the
complement to the locus of vanishing of $f$, and $M$ a holonomic D-module on
$U$.
  Consider the $D_U[s]$-module $M\otimes "f^s"$. The goal of this note is to
describe all $D_X[s]$-submodules $N\subset j_*(M\otimes "f^s")$ such that
$j^*(N)\simeq M\otimes "f^s"$.
