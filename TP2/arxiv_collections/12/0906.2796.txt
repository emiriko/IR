The present tutorial aims at covering the fundamentals of electromagnetism,
in a condensed and clear manner. Some solved and proposed exercises have been
included. The reader is assumed to have knowledge of basic electricity, partial
derivatives and multiple integrals.
  -----
  O presente tutorial visa cobrir os fundamentos do eletromagnetismo, de forma
condensada e clara. Alguns exercicios resolvidos e propostos foram incluidos.
Assume-se conhecimento de eletricidade basica, derivadas parciais e integrais
multiplas.
