The group classification of a class of semilinear reaction-diffusion
equations with exponential nonlinearity is carried out using the technique of
mapping between classes, which was recently proposed in [O.O. Vaneeva, R.O.
Popovych and C. Sophocleous, Acta Appl. Math., doi:10.1007/s10440-008-9280-9,
arXiv:0708.3457].
