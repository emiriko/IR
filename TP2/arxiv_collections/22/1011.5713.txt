PLM is today a reality for mechanical SMEs. Some companies implement PLM
systems very well but others have more difficulties. This paper aims to explain
why some SMEs do not success to integrated PLM systems analyzing the needs of
mechanical SMEs, the processes to implement to respond to those needs and the
actual PLM software functionalities. The proposition of a typology of those
companies and the responses of those needs by PLM processes will be explain
through the applications of a demonstrator applying appropriate generic data
model and modelling framework.
