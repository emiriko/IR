We report on analyses of tau lepton decays $\tau^- \to \eta K^- \nu_{\tau}$
and $\tau^- \to \eta \pi^- \nu_{\tau}$, with $\eta \to \pi^+ \pi^- \pi^0$,
using 470 fb$^{-1}$ of data from the Babar experiment at PEP-II, collected at
center-of-mass energies at and near the $\Upsilon(4S)$ resonance. We measure
the branching fraction for the $\tau^- \to \eta K^- \nu_{\tau}$ decay mode,
$\Br(\tau^- \to \eta K^- \nu_{\tau}) =
(1.42\pm0.11\text{(stat)}\pm0.07\text{(syst)})\times10^{-4}$, and report a 95%
confidence level upper limit for the second-class current process $\tau^- \to
\eta \pi^- \nu_{\tau}$, $\Br(\tau^- \to \eta \pi^- \nu_{\tau}) <
9.9\times10^{-5}$.
